Technology

My opinions and usage of technology are rigid after several years of change. Before entering high school, I switched my main device to Arch Linux which was the main factor in forming my opinions.

As of now, I restrict myself to solely using a laptop running void linux and use jailed, outdated iOS on my phone in order to make it as “dumb” as possible.

My git is currently hosted on disroot which can be viewed here.
